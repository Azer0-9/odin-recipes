Fried Boursin Balls

description

Fried boursin cheese in a small bite-sized package! These fun appetizers are crunchy on the outside, with a delicious warm herby center.

ingredients

1 quart canola oil for frying, or as needed 1 cup all-purpose flour 2 large eggs, beaten 1 cup Italian seasoned breadcrumbs 2 (4.23-oz) packages Boursin Garlic and Fine Herbs Cheese Bites
