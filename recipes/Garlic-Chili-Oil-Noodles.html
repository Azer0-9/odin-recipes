Garlic Chili Oil Noodles

description

These easy garlic chili oil noodles are not for those with a mild palate! Wok-fried garlic chili oil takes ramen noodles to a new level.

ingredients

1 package ramen noodles 2 teaspoons sesame oil 2 teaspoons soy sauce 1 tablespoon chiu chow style chili oil 4 cloves garlic, minced 1 pinch black sesame seeds 2 green onions, sliced diagonally, or to taste
